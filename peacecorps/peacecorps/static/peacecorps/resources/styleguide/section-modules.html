3Modules #permalink

Modules are larger, more specific resuable pieces that style specific parts of the site, such as the logo or main navigation.

3.1Article #permalink

A class for styling text and image articles, used for project pages currently.

Example

Some heading

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed sem mauris , tempor eget pretium at, interdum id ante. Donec a consectetur orci. Nullam in neque vel libero interdum egestas. Donec laoreet dui eu consequat laoreet. 

<section class="article">
  <h3>Some heading</h3>
  <div class="text-block">
    <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed sem mauris
     , tempor eget pretium at, interdum id ante. Donec a consectetur orci.
     Nullam in neque vel libero interdum egestas. Donec laoreet dui eu
       consequat laoreet.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>
